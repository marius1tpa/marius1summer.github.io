Key Information

Our summer camp is open to children aged 7-14. It will be held at the beautiful XYZ Nature Resort, surrounded by stunning natural landscapes.

  • Duration: 2 weeks
  • Activities: Hiking, swimming, arts and crafts, team-building, and more
  • Dates: July 15 - July 30, 2023
  • Price: $500 per child

Program Overview

Our summer camp offers a diverse range of activities that provide a perfect balance of fun, education, and personal growth. Some highlights include:

  • Morning hikes exploring the surrounding nature
  • Swimming lessons and water sports in our private lake
  • Arts and crafts workshops to ignite creativity
  • Team-building games and challenges
  • Campfire nights with storytelling and s'mores
  • Special theme days and exciting surprise events

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I visit my child during the camp?
For security and safety reasons, parents are not allowed to visit during the camp. However, we provide regular updates and photos to keep you informed.
What should my child bring to the camp?
Children should bring comfortable clothing, swimwear, sunscreen, a water bottle, and personal toiletries. A detailed packing list will be provided upon registration.
Is financial aid available?
We offer a limited number of scholarships and financial aid options. Please contact our team for more information.
